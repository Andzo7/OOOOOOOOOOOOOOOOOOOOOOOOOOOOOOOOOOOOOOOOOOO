About Us

We are a dynamic property investment and management company with a strong presence in Ireland and Greece. Our mission is to transform real estate investments into thriving assets, offering hassle-free and profitable property ownership to our clients.

At Andzo Properties Ltd, we treat every property as if it were our own. Our expertise, dedication, and stress-free management approach ensure your investments thrive while you enjoy peace of mind.

Currently, we operate in Ireland and Greece, where we have built a reputation for excellence in property acquisition, renovation, and management. We are excited to announce our plans to expand our operations into the United States, bringing our proven strategies and commitment to quality to new markets.

Whether you're looking to invest in property or need expert management for your existing portfolio, Andzo Properties Ltd is your trusted partner in real estate.
